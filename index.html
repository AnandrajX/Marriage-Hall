"Where every celebration becomes an unforgettable chapter in your family's story."

Nestled in the heart of Bhiti, Mau, Pratap Palace has been the region's most trusted and revered marriage venue for over a decade. Our sprawling halls, magnificent décor, and dedicated staff ensure that every wedding, reception, and celebration exceeds the highest expectations.

From the grandest royal baraats to the most intimate mehendi ceremonies, we curate every detail with precision, warmth, and an unwavering commitment to your happiness.
